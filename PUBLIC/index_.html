Rolling Roadblock Announced For New Circle Road

Posted: Updated:

LEXINGTON, Ky. (LEX 18) –  A temporary rolling roadblock is scheduled for New Circle Road Tuesday evening into Wednesday morning.

The Kentucky Transportation Cabinet says the mobile operation is necessary for revisions to be made on traffic counting devices within the asphalt.

The roadblock will begin at 7 p.m. Tuesday night and will last until 7 a.m. Wednesday on the inner and outer loops between Versailles Road and Leestown Road.

Motorists should expect delays during the scheduled hours of work.
